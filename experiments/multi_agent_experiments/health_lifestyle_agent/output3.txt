### Recipe: Mediterranean Turkey Skillet

**Ingredients:**
- 1 pound ground turkey (lean)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bell pepper, chopped
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, no salt added
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
- Fresh parsley for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until translucent.
2. Add ground turkey and cook until browned.
3. Stir in bell pepper and zucchini; cook for about 5 minutes.
4. Add diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Top with feta cheese and garnish with parsley before serving.

### Health Alignment:
- **Low Carbohydrate and Glycemic Load:** Helps in maintaining stable blood sugar levels.
- **Heart-Healthy Ingredients:** Lean turkey and olive oil support cardiovascular health.
- **High Fiber and Nutrient-Rich Vegetables:** Zucchini and bell peppers provide fiber and essential nutrients.
- **Sodium Control:** Use of no-salt-added tomatoes and minimal added salt aligns with low-sodium recommendations.

This recipe is appropriate given your medications and lifestyle recommendations, such as maintaining a low-sodium, heart-healthy diet and ensuring glycemic control. If you need further customization or have specific dietary restrictions, let me know!
